My Achievement

I participated in STEMFEST 2.0 held on 11th November 2023, a multidisciplinary event that brought together students from various schools to engage in scientific innovation and technical challenges.

I earned a Certificate of Participation for my involvement and was also honored with a Shield as the Winner of the Marble Maneuvers Competition — a highly competitive event demanding precision engineering, logical thinking, and strategic design.
